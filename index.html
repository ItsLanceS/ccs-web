About CCS Student Council

Our Mission

To serve as the official voice of all Computer Studies students, advocating for their academic, social, and professional needs while fostering a collaborative and inclusive learning environment.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant student community where every CCS student feels heard, supported, and empowered to achieve their academic and career goals in technology.

What We Do

  • Represent student interests in academic and administrative matters
  • Organize student activities and networking events
  • Facilitate communication between students and faculty
  • Provide academic support and mentorship programs
  • Address student concerns and feedback
  • Promote student welfare and campus life improvements
